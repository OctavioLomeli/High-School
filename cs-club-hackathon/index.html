Code Switching

Description

In this website, you will talk with a bot. The bot has three ways of speaking. It can speak academically, casually, or with a lot of slang. By the end of the game, we hope that you learned how to code switch.

Definitions

Academic - The type of English you would see in essays or professional settings.
Casually - The type of English you would speak with a friend or family.
Slang - A more 'extreme' version of casual. It sometimes gives new definitions for words.
Code switching - The process of shifting from one linguistic code to another, depending on the social context or conversational setting.

Instructions

The bot will say something that is either slang, casual, or academic. You will be provided four sentences to choose from to respond with. Each response will provide either -1, -.5, .5 or 1 point. Your goal is to decide which sentence best matches the type of English the bot spoke with.

You can win by getting 6 points. You can lose by getting -3 points or failing to get 6 points within 12 conversations. Your points will be displayed on the screen.
